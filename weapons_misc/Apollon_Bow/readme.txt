'Apollon' Bow (Greek: 'Apollyon', 'Destroyer') is a powerful weapon that was used by Ikaros, a character of an anime named 'Heaven's Lost Property' (Sora no Otoshimono). According to legend this bow was used in the destruction of the Tower of Babel.
