:::::::Killing Floor Team Strikes:::::::




Unzip the contents of the zip into this folder C:\Program Files\Steam\steamapps\common\killingfloor\
and enjoy!

About:

This a mutator/gametype that allows you to load a map into the Killing Strike game mode with a prefix of "DM-". 
Be aware of the fact that the map loaded should be made to fit the gameplay and is not for the
stock coop game mode.

Gameplay: You and your team are face another wipe the other team out and win points, or every kill 
and round won you get money and the buy menu comes up to select your weapon for the round.
